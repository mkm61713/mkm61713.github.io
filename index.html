There are three important things I learned that I need to use in order to become a better photographer and optimize my photo taking skills. They are aperture., ISO, and shutter speed. This table explains what they are:

What it does
ApertureThis refers to how much light you let in to the camera. This is measured in f-stops. If you have a high f-stop, you are letting in less light, and thus you will have more of the photograph in focus. With a smaller f-stop, you are letting in less light and only focusing on one object.
ExposureThis refers to how much light your shot is exposed to.
Shutter SpeedThis refers to how long the shutter is left open. There are fast shutter speeds which allow you to take action shots. There are long exposure shots that can take a minute long which people use for landscapes, such as waterfalls.
