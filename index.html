Photography

Photography is the art of taking photos. I started taking photos back in middle school and have continued doing it until today. My favorite type of photography is nature. The type of camera I own is a Canon Rebel T3i. ... There are three important things you need to know about the camera in order to maximize your photo taking skills.

What it's calledWhat it does
AperatureThis refers to how much light you let in to the camera. This is measured in f-stops. If you have a high f-stop, you are letting in less light, and thus you will have more of the photograph in focus. With a smaller f-stop, you are letting in less light and only focusing on one object.
ExposureThis refers to how much light your shot is exposed to.
Shutter SpeedThis refers to how long the shutter is left open. There are fast shutter speeds which allow you to take action shots. There are long exposure shots that can take a minute long which people use for landscapes, such as waterfalls.
